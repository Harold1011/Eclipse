    • ABOUT US


    We have an ambitious goal to reach a 25% reduction in carbon emissions across the full value chain, including all downstream and upstream activities, which means a total of 7million metric tons of CO2. This accounts for more than half of our total carbon footprint.

    We started the journey, but we can’t reach our goal alone, we need the help of our suppliers, sharing best practices and finding opportunities through collaboration to make it a reality.

    As Partners of AB InBev, we set our goals and made our pledge, and now we’re asking you to make a commitment too. Through program Eclipse, a platform that connects AB InBev with its suppliers, together we can affect positive climate change and the creation of a better world.
